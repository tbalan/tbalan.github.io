About

I’m a data scientist with a background in biostatistics and mathematics. I’m interested in survival analysis, causal inference and machine learning. I mostly code in R, and am the author and maintainer of the frailtyEM package. I have a PhD degree from Leiden University based on methodological research in frailty models.

I’ve worked in academia at the Leiden University Medical Center from 2013 to 2018. Since 2019 I work as a data scientist at Danone Nutricia Research in development of solutions for early life nutrition and medical foods.

For more info you can send me an email, check out some of my code on Github, my work & education history on LinkedIn, my peer-reviewed publications on Google Scholar or my occasional blog posts on Medium.
