Biography

I am a PhD student in the department of Medical Statistics and Bioinformatics at Leiden University Medical Center. My work is centered around complex time-to-event data, mostly in the field of random effect (frailty models) in survival analysis. In my spare time, I like to put my skills to use for good causes. I am the co-author and maintainer of the R packages frailtyEM and dynfrail.
